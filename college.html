LIST IN HTML

ORDERED LIST

An ordered list in HTML is a list of items where the sequence or order of the items is significant. It is used when the arrangement of the list items is important, such as in step-by-step instructions, rankings, or chronological events

  1. numeric
  2. capital alphabet
  3. small alphabet

UNORDERED LIST

An unordered list in HTML is used to present a collection of items where the order of the items is not significant. These lists are typically displayed with bullet points by default.

  • disc
  • square
  • circle

DISCRIPTION LIST

An HTML description list, also known as a definition list, is used to display a list of terms and their corresponding descriptions or definitions. It is particularly useful for presenting glossaries, FAQs, or any content where a term is followed by its explanation.

coffee
- black hot drink
milk
- white cold drink
